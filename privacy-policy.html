Backdrop Me — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Backdrop Me is a wallpaper maker. Your wallpapers are created on your device and stay there. With your consent, the app sends anonymous usage analytics to help us improve it. You choose this on first launch, and you can change it anytime in the app's About screen.

What we collect

If you turn analytics on, Backdrop Me uses Mixpanel, an analytics service, to understand which features are used so we can improve the app. The following is sent to Mixpanel:

  • Anonymous identifier — a random ID generated for your device by the analytics library. It is not your name, email, phone number, or any account — Backdrop Me has no accounts and never asks who you are.
  • Usage events — e.g. app opened, a gradient preset selected, colors adjusted, a quote chosen, a font selected, image saved, wallpaper set, image shared — together with the design choices those actions describe (for example the gradient colors, font, style settings, and date sticker used).
  • Caption text you write — only if you separately opt in (a checkbox on the first-launch notice, or a toggle in About; off by default). When it is on, the words you type onto a wallpaper are included in the analytics event. When it is off, we still record that you wrote your own words — their length and script (for example Latin or Chinese) — but never the words themselves.
  • Device context — device model, operating system version, app version, and similar technical properties attached automatically by the analytics library. Your IP address is processed on receipt and may yield an approximate (city-level) location; we do not collect precise location.

Messages you send us

The app's About screen has an optional "send me a message" box. If you write a message and tap Send, the text you typed is delivered to us through Mixpanel, together with the anonymous device identifier — nothing else. This happens only when you tap Send, and it works independently of the analytics setting above: tapping Send is your consent for that message. Please don't include personal details you wouldn't want us to see.

What we don't collect

  • No name, email address, phone number, contacts, or account of any kind.
  • No precise location.
  • No photos: the wallpapers you create are saved to your own gallery (Pictures/Backdrop) and are never uploaded anywhere.
  • No advertising identifiers — Backdrop Me contains no ads.
  • No automatic screen recording or session replay.

Who we share data with

Analytics data is processed and stored by Mixpanel, Inc. (United States) on our behalf — see Mixpanel's privacy policy. We do not sell data, and we do not share it with anyone else.

Retention & deletion

Analytics data is retained only as long as needed to understand app usage. You can request deletion of the data associated with your device at any time by emailing us — we will work with Mixpanel to remove it.

Children

Backdrop Me is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date.

Backdrop Me — 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 11 日

Backdrop Me 是一款壁纸制作应用。你的壁纸在你的设备上生成并保存在本地。在你同意后， 应用会发送匿名使用统计以帮助我们改进。你在首次启动时选择是否开启，也可以随时在应用的 「关于」页面更改。

我们收集什么

若你开启统计，Backdrop Me 会使用 Mixpanel 分析服务来 了解各功能的使用情况，以便改进应用。发送给 Mixpanel 的内容包括：

  • 匿名标识符 —— 由分析库为你的设备生成的随机 ID。它不是你的姓名、 邮箱、手机号或任何账号 —— Backdrop Me 没有账号体系，也从不询问你的身份。
  • 使用事件 —— 例如：打开应用、选择渐变预设、调整颜色、选用语录、 选择字体、保存图片、设置壁纸、分享图片，以及这些操作所对应的设计选择（例如所用的 渐变颜色、字体、样式设置和日期贴纸）。
  • 你输入的文字 —— 仅在你单独选择开启时（首次启动提示中的勾选项，或 「关于」中的开关；默认关闭）。开启后，你写在壁纸上的文字会随对应的统计事件一并发送。 关闭时，我们仍会记录你写下了自己的文字——其长度与文种（例如拉丁文或中文）——但不会 记录文字内容本身。
  • 设备信息 —— 设备型号、系统版本、应用版本等由分析库自动附带的 技术属性。你的 IP 地址在接收时会被处理，可能得出大致（城市级）位置；我们不收集 精确位置。

你发给我们的留言

应用的「关于」页面提供可选的「给我留言」输入框。当你写下留言并点按「发送」时，你输入的 文字会连同匿名设备标识符一起通过 Mixpanel 发送给我们——仅此而已。只有在你点按「发送」时 才会发送，且不受上述统计开关的影响：点按「发送」即表示你同意发送这条留言。请不要在留言中 包含你不希望我们看到的个人信息。

我们不收集什么

  • 不收集姓名、邮箱、手机号、通讯录，也没有任何账号。
  • 不收集精确位置。
  • 不上传照片：你制作的壁纸只保存在你自己的相册（Pictures/Backdrop）， 不会被上传到任何地方。
  • 无广告标识符 —— Backdrop Me 没有广告。
  • 无自动屏幕录制或会话回放。

数据共享

统计数据由 Mixpanel, Inc.（美国）代表我们处理和存储 —— 参见 Mixpanel 隐私政策。我们不出售 数据，也不与其他任何方共享。

保留与删除

统计数据仅在了解应用使用情况所需的期间内保留。你可以随时发邮件请求删除与你的设备 相关的数据，我们会配合 Mixpanel 予以删除。

儿童

Backdrop Me 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们也不会有意收集他们的个人信息。

政策变更

如本政策有变更，新版本将发布在本页面，并更新生效日期。
